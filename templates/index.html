About

Sangita Roy, is an avid nature lover, literature enthusiast and a qualified plant breeder. Her novel Zoey & The Spirit of Juniper gained notable recognition at the World Book Fair and the prestigious Frankfurt Book Fair 2024, marking a significant milestone in her literary journey. Her prose and poetries have been widely published in national and international journals and anthologies. She continues refining her works that linger in the minds of readers, leaving echoes of intrigue and wonder.
